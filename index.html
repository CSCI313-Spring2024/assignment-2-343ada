Allen Hastings

Software Engineering


My name is Allen Hastings. I am originally from New Mexico. I was born and raised on the Navajo Nation, of which I am an enrolled member. My children are enrolled members of the Navajo Nation Tribe as well. My Wife is a member of the Standing Rock Sioux Tribe.Aside from being a father and a husband I am also a Veteran of the United States Army and a student here at NDSU. Before becoming a student I had a career working in the Texas and NM oilfields.

Today I live here in Fargo ND with my wife Kandice and my two youngest children. My daughter Kaimyn and my youngest son Arik shown here holding my beautiful one and only granddaughter.

My older 3 children are grown and out living their lives.My oldest son Kheyanev my daughter and Army mom Khadeighjah with her daughter Kamari and finally my oldest daughter Jocelyn holding yet again my beautiful one and only granddaughter..

My Education


Shiprock High is where I recieved my High School diploma, it is located a small town on the Navajo Nation reservation in New Mexico

Sitting Bull College in Fort Yates ND, is located on the Standing Rock Soiux reservation
This is where I recieved my Associates degree in Pre-Engineering

NDSU is where I am currently taking classes where I will recieve by bachelor's degree in Software Engineering
